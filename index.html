What We Do

A: Defence Initiative focuses on making Mindanao and the Philippines safer by addressing key issues like terrorism, poverty, and instability. We provide aid, build community resilience, and support local security forces to combat terrorism.

  • Counterterrorism: We assist in efforts to protect Mindanao from terrorism and insurgencies.
  • Humanitarian Aid: We offer food, shelter, and medical care to families affected by conflict.
  • Community Empowerment: We help communities grow by providing education and job training to build resilience.

Our mission is to improve security and quality of life in Mindanao and across the Philippines.
