What We Do

We focus on counterterrorism, providing humanitarian aid, and improving security in Mindanao. Our goal is to build safer communities, reduce poverty, and promote peace in the region.

  • Counterterrorism: Supporting local law enforcement to combat terrorism and insurgency.
  • Humanitarian Aid: Offering food, medical care, and shelter to displaced families.
  • Community Empowerment: Providing education and vocational training to promote local development.

Our mission is to build a safer, more prosperous future for Mindanao and the Philippines.
