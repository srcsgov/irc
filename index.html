What We Do

At A: Defence Initiative, we are committed to making a lasting difference in Mindanao, a region that has been severely affected by conflict, poverty, and insecurity. We focus on counterterrorism efforts, providing humanitarian aid, and working toward improving the overall security and stability of the area. Our vision is to build safer communities, empower local people, and address the root causes of violence and poverty.

Our work includes:

  • Counterterrorism Operations: We support national and local law enforcement agencies in combating terrorism and insurgency in the region, ensuring that security forces are equipped and trained to protect innocent lives.
  • Humanitarian Aid and Relief: We provide essential services, such as food, medical care, and shelter, to communities that have been displaced by violence. Our efforts help rebuild lives and restore hope to those who have been affected by conflict.
  • Community Empowerment: We work closely with local communities to strengthen their resilience against extremism and promote social cohesion. This includes educational programs, vocational training, and economic development initiatives that foster sustainable growth and independence.

Our mission is driven by the belief that security, peace, and prosperity can be achieved when communities work together with their governments and international partners to address the challenges they face.

How to Join Us

If you share our vision of a peaceful, secure, and prosperous Mindanao, we invite you to join us in our mission. We operate in various regions across the Philippines, and our work is not limited to one specific area. We value flexibility, commitment, and a passion for helping others, and we're always looking for dedicated individuals to join our cause.

There are many ways you can contribute to our efforts:

  • Volunteer: We welcome passionate volunteers to assist with our programs, outreach, and community initiatives. Whether you have experience in humanitarian aid, security, or other fields, there's a place for you in our team.
  • Apply for Open Roles: We frequently have positions available, including roles in field operations, administrative support, communications, and more. If you're interested in a specific role or want to explore opportunities with us, simply send us your resume and a brief cover letter detailing the role you'd like to apply for. We will review your application and get in touch with you if there's a suitable match.
  • Inquire About Open Roles: If you're unsure about what roles we currently have available, feel free to reach out to us to inquire. We are always open to discussing potential opportunities based on your skills and interests.

To get involved, please send your resume and the role you're interested in (or inquire about open roles) to: a.defenceinitiative@gmail.com

We believe that with the right team, we can make a significant impact. Join us in our mission to build a safer, more secure Philippines, and help us create a brighter future for all.
